A Short Bio

My name is Sean Madigan. I attended the University of Utah receiveing my Bachelor's in Kinesiology. During the pursuit of that degree I beleived I would have a career in Medicine or Physical Therapy. Upon the completion of my degree I realized that I disliked the costs and inflexibility of my career path and decided to pivot to business. Inorder to strengthen my medically inclined resume I pursued and received an MBA from Western Governor's University. During that program I did some soul searching and found myself with an opportunity to take an intro to coding course and fell in love with Web Development. I then pursued a coding course offered by a partnership between the University of Utah and EdX. This course has equipped me with all the necessary tools and skills to be a effective team member and full stack developer.

Professionally, I have a varied and diverse background that makes me highly adaptable. I have worked in child-care, mental health, emergency medicine, the restaurant and service industry, and vehicle sales in the pursuit of my passion. All of my prior experience has lead me to web development and I am excited to begin a long successful career in this industry.

More personally, I hail from Boise, Idaho originally, but spent many years growing up in Europe. During that time I lived in Ireland, Spain, Switzerland, and France. As a result of my time abroad I speak English, Spanish, French, and German and consider myself a bit of an international mutt. I am an avid surfer, dancer, climber, skiier/snowboarder, scuba diver, and most importanly a devoted husband. My wife and I have two fur babies named Juno and Dolores. I am a lover of good food, nights out, and authentic high quality interactions.
